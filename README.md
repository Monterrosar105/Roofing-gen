# Landscaping Business Website Template

This is a professional website template for landscaping businesses. It includes responsive design, modern styling, and all the essential sections for a landscaping service website.

## Features

- Responsive design that works on all devices
- Professional styling with green color scheme
- Services section with icons
- Portfolio showcase
- Contact information with phone and WhatsApp integration
- About section highlighting key features

## Customization Guide

To customize this template for your business, replace the following placeholders throughout the `index.html` file:

### Required Replacements

1. **[Your Business Name]** - Replace with your company name
   - Appears in title, meta description, header, alt texts, and footer

2. **[Your City, State]** - Replace with your location
   - Used in the page title

3. **[Your Service Areas]** - Replace with your service locations
   - Used in meta description, about section, portfolio description, and contact section

4. **[Your Region]** - Replace with your general service region
   - Used in the about section description

5. **[Your Phone Number]** - Replace with your phone number (without formatting)
   - Used in tel: links, WhatsApp links, and display text
   - Format: 1234567890 (10 digits, no parentheses or dashes)

### Image Replacements

Replace the following images in the `images/` folder:

- `Logo.png` - Your company logo
- `hero.jpg` - Hero background image
- `team.jpg` - Team photo for about section
- Images in `portfolio/` folder - Your work samples
- Images in `services/` folder - Service icons
- Images in `bullets/` folder - Feature icons

### Optional Customizations

- Update the copyright year in the footer
- Modify service descriptions to match your offerings
- Add or remove portfolio items
- Adjust the color scheme in `styles/main.css` if desired

## Getting Started

1. Clone or download this repository
2. Replace all placeholders as described above
3. Replace the images with your own
4. Customize the content to match your business
5. Upload to your web hosting provider
